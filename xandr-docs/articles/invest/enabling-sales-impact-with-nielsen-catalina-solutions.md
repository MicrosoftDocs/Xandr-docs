# Enabling Sales Impact for Line Item Delivery with Nielsen Catalina Solutions



If you activate Nielsen's Sales Impact tracking for your line item, you
can analyze how your served creatives have influenced physical, in-store
product purchases among your target audiences.

Sales Impact through Nielsen Catalina Solutions (NCS) is a measurement
capability that advertisers promoting consumer packaged goods can use to
understand how their line item delivery is impacting offline sales.



<b>Note:</b> Sales Impact only works when
targeting United States audiences.



Enabling Sales Impact adds a $0.55 CPM fee for served impressions, and
you gain access to the
<a href="offline-attribution-report.md" class="xref"
title="The Offline Attribution report lets you track your line item&#39;s offline sales performance. This report is only available for Microsoft Invest clients that have Offline Attribution enabled on their line items.">Offline
Attribution Report</a>, which you can generate at customized intervals.
For information on enabling NCS Sales Impact, see
<a href="activate-nielsen-sales-impact-for-a-line-item.md"
class="xref"
title="At $0.55 CPM for served impressions, you can activate Nielsen Catalina Solutions&#39; (NCS) Sales Impact measurement for your augmented line item. Sales Impact lets you analyze your line item&#39;s performance in influencing offline purchases among your target audience.">Activate
Nielsen Sales Impact for a Line Item</a>.
