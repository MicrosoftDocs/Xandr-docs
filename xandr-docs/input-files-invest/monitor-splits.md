# Monitor Splits



You can monitor delivery and performance for your splits.






1.  Click the row for the appropriate line item
    from the Line Items
    grid.
2.  From the View
    Line Item Details pane, click the
    Analytics tab.
3.  Scroll to the
    Programmable Splits section
    where you can analyze how your splits are delivering and performing
    based on the available metrics.
    

    All splits that have been set up on the line item will appear in the
    Programmable Splits section.
