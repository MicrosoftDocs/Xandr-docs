# Updating My Profile



Log in to the
<a href="https://help.xandr.com" class="xref" target="_blank">Customer
Support Portal</a> to access your full case history, knowledge base
articles, or request support.

Your Customer Support Portal profile includes your name and photo.  

To view your profile click on the
Profile menu and select
My Profile.

<figure class="fig fignone">
<p><img src="../images/xcs/xcs14.png" class="image" width="300" /></p>
</figure>

Click on your profile photo to upload a new photo, or update your
current one.
